Art

I love to be creative and I mainly draw and paint. I always have a sketchbook on me at all times in case I see something that inspires me. I mainly use blue pen but I know how to use many mediums like paint, pencils, marker, colored pencil, ink, and even digital tools. I use pen and paper and my IPad to draw most often. With traditional mediums I mainly doodle and make sketches, but with my ipad I usually make finished artworks.
